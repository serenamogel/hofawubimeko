Pokmon Detective Pikachu Original Motion Picture Soundtrack

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Pokémon Detective Pikachu Original Motion Picture Soundtrack (Japanese: 『名探偵ピカチュウ』オリジナル・サウンドトラック Great Detective Pikachu Original Soundtrack) is the soundtrack for the movie POKÉMON Detective Pikachu by Henry Jackman. It was released digitally on May 3, 2019, on CD on May 10, 2019, and on vinyl on July 26, 2019. The limited edition vinyl release includes a movie poster, four-page booklet, exclusive postcard, and a picture of Detective Pikachu on the D-side. In Japan, it was released physically on CD on May 29, 2019 with the exclusive bonus track "Midnight Demo". The only piece of music that was not included was Jackman's end credits arrangement of the original Pokémon Red, Green, Blue, and Yellow theme composed by Junichi Masuda.
