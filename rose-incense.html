Rose Incense
おはなのおこう
Flower Incense
Pokémon Global Link artwork
Introduced in Generation IV

The Rose Incense (Japanese: おはなのおこう Flower Incense) is a type of held item introduced in Generation IV. It is an incense that allows Roselia and Roserade to produce Budew Eggs. In battle, its effect is identical to the Miracle Seed.

In the core series games

Price

Effect

In battle

Boosts the power of the holder's Grass-type moves by 20%.

Outside of battle

If held by either parent of a pairing in Pokémon Day Care or the Pokémon Nursery that would produce a Roselia Egg, a Budew Egg is produced instead.
