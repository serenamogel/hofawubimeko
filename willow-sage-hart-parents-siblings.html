Who Is Willow Sage Hart?

Willow Sage Hart is a ten-year-old beautiful girl and famous for being the daughter of Pink and Carey Hart. She is definitely close to her mom, who calls and considers her twin. Her mother, Pink, chose her name, and she shared Willow was her favorite tree as it is the most flexible tree, and she wanted her daughter to be like it.

Willow has appeared in a duet with her singer mother, Pink, on Cover Me In Sunshine. Willow also showcased her singing skills on The Greatest Showman: Reumagined album in 2018, and along with that, she performed on ANC's airing of The Disney Holiday Singalong in 2020. She also mesmerized the viewers of Nat King Cole's The Christmas Song. 

However, Pink has shared her daughter, Willow, has no plans to be a singer in the future.

Pink had also described her children, Willow and Jameson, as weird and joyful. Pink had also gone on her tour Beautiful Trauma World Tour along with her family/

Willow Sage Hart Parents Details

Willow Sage Hart was born to celebrity parents Pink and Carey Hart. They recently celebrated their 16th wedding anniversary. They got married in 2006 after dating in 2001. Pink has talked about how her 15 years of marriage were successful because of counseling.

Willow's parents have had on and off relationships in the past. They broke up two times before welcoming her and her brother, Jameson.

The couple solidified their marriage after getting marriage counseling.
