Who Owns Del Records?

Music continues to evolve and has moved from just a recreational exercise to a whole business model and record labels today are an important part of an artist’s journey to the top. The choice of deciding what label an artist joins is a very delicate one as this can make or unmake an artist, that’s the relevance record labels hold today. Record labels are responsible for the funding that comes with a music project from the production of videos, beats and marketing are all a few but the many things record labels take care of on behalf of artists signed to them.

In today’s music scene, we have such powerful recording labels as Sony Music Entertainment, Universal Music Group, Atlantic Records, Interscope records and so many more. It’s a category Del records equally fall under and one that has been the most successful in the Latino diaspora.

Who Owns Del Records?

Del Records is owned by music executive Angel del Villar and the record label was founded in 2009.
