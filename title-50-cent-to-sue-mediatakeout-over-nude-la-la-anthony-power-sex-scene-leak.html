50 Cent To Sue MediaTakeOut Over Nude La La Anthony Power Sex Scene Leak

50 Cent has reacted to MediaTakeOut posting a sex scene featuring La La Anthony from a forthcoming episode of the television show Power, which he executive produces and stars in.

“Shout out to MediaTakeOut for showing La La some love and showing her scene early,” 50 Cent says in a video posted on ForbezDVDPromo’s YouTube page. “For doing that, I’m not gonna take it personal, so don’t take it personal with me when they sue you til you’re blue in the face.”

The article has since been removed from MediaTakeOut.
