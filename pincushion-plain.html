Pincushion Plain

Pincushion Plain (Japanese: はりやま原野 Pincushion Plain) is a stage in Pokémon Quest that is located in Tumblecube Island. It is unlocked once the player completes all stages in Gloomy Grove and Backforth Brook. Completing all stages of Pincushion Plain rewards the player with the Abundance Statue which increases experience gained from expeditions by 1.5 times for Pokémon up to Lv. 30.

Bug-type Pokémon have a boost in their HP and Attack when playing these stages.
