Ankur Rathee is a multi-skilled person who is known for his acting prowess and dancing skills. He is an Indian- American artist who flaunts his extraordinary skills that is enough to steal the hearts of millions of his fans. He is mainly known for his roles in the Amazon Prime web series “Four More Shots Please” which was released in 2019. Also, he is popular for the role played in another web series “Made in Heaven” directed by Zoya Akhtar and released in 2019. He was born to Usha Rathee and Joginder Singh Rathee on 24th March 1991.

Career

The young actor, the dancer has worked in a large number of movies and has been enthralling his fans with amazing performances. In 2019, he has appeared in two most-watched web series including “Made in Heaven” by Zoya Akhtar and another one is “Four More Shots Please”. For these roles, he got immense love and much recognition from fans and viewers. Moreover, he has worked in the Bollywood industry and has appeared in various movies including “Thappad” which is set to release in 2020 in which he worked alongside Dia Mirza. Earlier, he worked in “The Tashkent Files” which was released in 2019. While in the film, he worked alongside the veteran actor, Naseeruddin Shah.

Apart from acting, he is blessed with amazing dance skills and has won the hearts of many with his breath-taking performance on the Dance India Dance show which was aired in 2009.

He did his graduation from Princeton University where he got trained under the supervision of trained artists, like; John Rando, who is a Tony Award victor, Leigh Silverman, and others. Later, he studied filmmaking from USC School of Cinematic Arts.

After moving to New York City, he worked as a theatre artist and appeared in various plays. Some of his worth sharing performances are “Jade Emperor”, “Journey Beyond the West” and others are included.

In 2018, he appeared on the stage alongside “Ila Arun and K.K. Raina in a rendition named “Baby’s Blues”.

The most talented, heartthrob artist started his dance training at the age of 8, and he learned various forms of dance from Bollywood, hip-hop, jazz to the aerobatic adage, and others. Also, he was so passionate about acting and singing as well and he made his career in these multiple fields. A very few people know that he is a multilingual man and can speak Hindi, English, and Haryanvi languages.

Achievements

Among various prestigious achievements and honors, Ankur Rathee became a member of the Dance Faculty at “Steps on Broadway”.
