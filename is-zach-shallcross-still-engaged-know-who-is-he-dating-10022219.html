Who is Zach Shallcross?

Zachary Lloyd Shallcross, born on July 31, 1996, is an American television personality who starred in season 27 of The Bachelor and appeared on season 19 of The Bachelorette. Shallcross graduated from California Polytechnic State University in 2019 and mentioned in an Instagram post that his college experience was completely worth it. Although he is originally from California, Shallcross is currently based in Austin, Texas, but also spends time in Orange County.

In addition to his professional life, Shallcross enjoys an active lifestyle and is a fan of beach volleyball and football, having played college football while attending Cal Poly.

According to his LinkedIn profile, Shallcross pursued a degree in business administration from California Polytechnic State University-San Luis Obispo and completed his studies from 2014 to 2019. About the Bachelor

The Bachelor is a popular reality TV show that has been airing on ABC since 2002. The show follows a single man who is looking for love among a group of women who compete for his affection. The Bachelor typically starts with around 25 contestants, and each week he goes on dates with some of them while others are sent home.

Throughout the season, the Bachelor must make difficult decisions about which women to keep and which to let go. He is supported by the show's host and often receives advice from former Bachelor or Bachelorette contestants. The season typically ends with the Bachelor proposing to his final choice during a dramatic finale.

The show has been a cultural phenomenon and has launched the careers of several successful couples, including Trista and Ryan Sutter, Ashley and J.P. Rosenbaum, and Sean and Catherine Lowe. However, not all relationships formed on the show have lasted, and some have ended in dramatic breakups.

The Bachelor has faced criticism for its portrayal of relationships and the way it commodifies love. However, it remains a popular show that draws in millions of viewers each season.
