Jon Fishman’s Early Life

Jon Fishman was born on November 7, 1960, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. He developed a passion for music at a young age and began playing the drums when he was just 12 years old. His early interest in music laid the foundation for his successful career.

Fishman met his future bandmates while studying at the University of Vermont. Together with Trey Anastasio, Mike Gordon, and Page McConnell, he formed the band Phish in 1983. The band quickly gained a following with their unique blend of rock, jazz, and other genres.
